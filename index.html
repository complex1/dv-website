EEG

EEG(electroencephalogram) is a test that measures and records the electrical activity of your brain. Special sensors are attached to your head and hooked by wires to a computer.

Timing:   Mon-Sat 9am-9pm, Sun Closed

ECG

The ECG(electrocardiogram) is used to detect abnormal heart rhythms and to investigate the cause of chest pains.

Timing:   Mon-Sat 9am - 9pm, Sun Closed

NCS

NCS (Nerve Conduction Studies) is a diagnostic procedure to assess the health of muscles and the nerve cells that control them (motor neurons).

Timing:   Mon-Sat 9am-9pm, Sun Closed
